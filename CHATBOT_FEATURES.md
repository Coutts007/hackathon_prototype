## ❓ Frequently Asked About the Assistant

**Q: Is the chatbot AI-powered?**
A: Yes! It uses sophisticated intent recognition and knowledge base system to provide intelligent responses.

**Q: Can it access my personal data?**
A: Yes, only to personalize responses. Your data is never shared.

**Q: What if my question isn't about HEFI?**
A: The chatbot is specialized for HEFI. For other topics, contact your utility directly.

**Q: How accurate are the responses?**
A: All information is based on the official HEFI system specification and verified against utility guidelines.

**Q: Can I appeal or dispute a response?**
A: Yes! Contact your utility's customer support with documentation. The assistant provides information, not official decisions.

## 🔗 When to Contact Support

While the assistant is comprehensive, contact your utility for:
- Official score appeals
- Billing disputes
- Account changes
- Verification of subsidy eligibility
- Technical issues with your meter
