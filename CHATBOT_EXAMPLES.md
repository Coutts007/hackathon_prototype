## Example 1: Understanding HEFI Basics

```
USER: "What is HEFI?"

ASSISTANT:
🏠 **HEFI (Household Energy Fairness Index)** is an AI-powered scoring system 
(0-100) that determines fair electricity tariffs.

**Why HEFI Exists:**
- Traditional tariffs only consider consumption, ignoring those who truly need support
- HEFI ensures vulnerable households receive maximum subsidies
- It balances equity with efficiency across the entire grid

**What HEFI Considers:**
- 💰 Income: Lower income = Higher priority for subsidies
- 👥 Household Size: Larger families get more support
- ⚡ Energy Dependency: Critical reliance on electricity (medical, cooking)
- 📊 Consumption Patterns: Unusual usage flags are analyzed

Your unique combination of these factors determines your score.

```

3. **Close vents**: Keep cool air in one room during AC usage

1. **Open windows at night**: Let natural ventilation replace AC
2. **Use ceiling fans**: More efficient than air conditioning

4. **Cook efficiently**: Use lids on pots, cook multiple dishes together

1. **Install solar panels**: Reduces grid dependence by 40-60%
2. **Buy energy-efficient appliances**: STAR-rated refrigerators, fans
3. **Insulate your home**: Reduces cooling/heating needs
4. **Install solar water heater**: Eliminates 30-40% of consumption

**For Your HEFI Score:**
✅ **Reduces bill** → May lower your consumption anomaly score
✅ **Solar installation** → Adds "green bonus" to your index
⚠️ **But remember:** Better to be honest about consumption—don't lie 
   to improve your score!

## Example 6: Quick FAQ

```
USER: "How do I appeal my HEFI score?"

ASSISTANT:
**Yes, you can appeal your HEFI score!** 🎯

**Appeals Process:**

**Step 1: Gather Evidence**
   - Income documents (last 3 months)
   - Medical reports (if claiming energy dependency)
   - Household documentation (birth certificates, etc.)
   - Utility bills showing your usage

**Step 2: Contact Your Utility**
   - Call HEFI Appeals Department
   - Visit local office
   - Email formal appeal request
   - Expected response: 5-7 business days

**Step 3: Present Your Case**
   - Explain discrepancies
   - Provide supporting evidence
   - Request review by human analyst
   - Appeals are FREE (no charges)

**Step 4: Decision**
   - Score may increase, decrease, or stay same
   - You'll get written explanation
   - New score applies to next billing cycle

**What Can Be Appealed:**
✅ Income calculation errors
✅ Household size discrepancies  
✅ Energy dependency not recognized
✅ Meter reading errors
✅ System processing errors

**What Cannot Be Appealed:**
❌ Consumption anomaly (unless meter is faulty)
❌ Honest data that results in lower score
❌ Score changes from life updates

**Success Factors:**
- Be honest and provide evidence
- Respond promptly to utility requests
- Include clear documentation
- Keep records of all communications

---
💡 Follow-up Questions You Might Ask:
1. What documents do I need for an appeal?
2. How long do appeals take?
3. Can I get temporary relief during appeal?
```
